DUSD is an USD-peged stable token that is issued on public blockchain, currently it is created on FAB blockchain, Ethereum blockchain as well as Biance BSC chain, it was issued by Exchangily LLC which is a decentraliced cryptocurrency exchange registered in Ohio, United States.

One DUSD in value is equals one US Dollar.

Purpose of DUSD: mainly for online cryptocurrency payment, for ether offline or online business.
