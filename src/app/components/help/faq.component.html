1. What are the essential knowledge do I need to know before using Exchangily ?

Exchangily is a fully decentralized open-source digital wallet and crypto trading platform. Users take the full responsibility for the security of their digital wallet and crypto assets. Specifically, the mnemonics and private keys are very important in protecting your Exchangily wallet. Users are in control with their Exchangily wallet and digital assets always with the mnemonics and private keys. You should never share your mnemonics and private keys with others otherwise you will lose all your digital assets with Exchangily.
In addition, users should understand that as decentralized wallets and decentralized crypto exchanges, all transactions will need a gas fee. For some cryptos like Bitcoin (BTC), Ether (ETH), FAB coin, Dogecoin (DOGE), etc. They all have their own separate blockchain, which is called maincoin. You not only need to use up some of these coins as gas to make any transactions of those coins, but also need the maincoin for transactions of any derivative coins on the mainchain.
For example, if you want to send USDT to your other wallet, you will need to have enough ETH as gas for the transaction based on Ether’s blockchain. The same with EXG, you will need some FAB as gas fee to make any transactions of EXG.
*Note: Exchangily is built on the Kanban blockchain, this Kanban takes FAB coin as the primary gas. Therefore, any transactions on Exhcangily will need FAB as gas. You cannot make any transactions or actions without FAB.

2. Why do I always have errors while transferring cryptos to Exchangily ?

Since the exchange needs to confirm the correspondence between the crypto transfer and the Exchangily wallet, it needs some time to complete the transaction. For example, if you want to transfer BTC and ETH to the exchange, and you transfer BTC firstly, you will need to wait for the transaction of BTC to complete before transferring ETH. It usually takes 30 minutes to 1 hour to complete each transaction.

3. The crypto has been withdrawn from my Exchangliy wallet, but why didn't it show up on the exchange?

We want to make sure each transaction is valid and correct, so the transaction chain usually needs to confirm the number of blocks on the original chain before processing the transaction. We appreciate your patience while depositing cryptos to the exchange. Usually, it takes half an hour to one hour to complete the transaction, but sometimes it may take longer up to two hours. So, if you don’t receive your transaction for more than two hours, go ahead and check if there is a button to confirm the deposit at the deposit button. Then click the button and enter your wallet password to confirm, and the exchange will receive the deposit immediately.

4. Why does the exchange notify me that there’s not enough ETH when I transfer USDT?

Exchangily is a decentralized wallet and exchange, so any transaction and action are carried out based on the chain. Since USDT is a token on the Ether blockchain, transferring any token on Ether including USDT requires a payment of gas fee to the Ether blockchain. Similarly, transferring coins on the Fast Access Blockchain such as EXG and DUSD requires a payment of FAB as gas fee. Whenever you transfer cryptocurrencies, you need to have the corresponding main chain coins in your wallet.

5. Why didn't I receive the USDT transfer to the exchange when I had enough ETH in my wallet?

Exchangily takes FAB coin as a gas fee. Any transaction or action on Exchangily needs to take some FAB. Either depositing cryptos to the exchange or making any buy-or-sell transactions, you need to have FAB as gas fee regardless of the type of cryptocurrencies you trade. If it didn’t process correctly, go ahead and check if you have enough FAB.

6. What is adding gas to the exchange, and how much do I need to add each time?

Adding a gas is to add FAB to the exchange as Exchangily takes FAB coin as a gas fee. You need to add gas before making any transaction or action on the exchange. For example, transfer cryptocurrencies to Exchangily. The amount of gas you need to add to the exchange depending on the frequency you will trade on Exchangily. Usually, a few FAB coins will cover for many transactions in a time. In addition, if you only use the Exchangily wallet and will not use the exchange, then you don’t really need to add gas.

7. Why didn’t I receive my crypto withdrawals from the exchange to my wallet?

The exchange blockchain usually performs a crypto withdrawal every half an hour while sometimes the terminal blockchain may get busy and will take longer time to proceed. It usually takes 40 minutes to complete the withdrawal, but sometimes it could take several hours or even days due to the third-party chain.

常见问题回答

基本问题

1. 使用亿币需要了解哪些必备的知识 ?

亿币是一个去中心化的开源钱包和交易所系统，用户必须知道，用户对自己的资产安全负有全部责任。具体来说，助记词及由助记词生成的私钥是安全的生命线，拥有助记词或私钥，就完全控制了资产，用户必须妥善安全地保管好它们，不得遗失，不得泄露，否则您将失去全部资产。
另外，用户应该明白，作为去中心化的钱包及交易所，一切操作都需支付燃料费。有些币，如比特币BTC, 以太币ETH, 发币FAB, 莱特币LTC, 狗狗币DOGE等，都有自己独立的区块链，称为主链币，不仅发送主链币需要消耗这些币作为燃料，发送任何主链上的派生币同样需要消耗主链币作为燃料。
例如，如果您想向另一个钱包发送USDT，您的钱包中必须有足够的ETH以支付以太坊网络的燃料费，同理，如果想发送亿币EXG，钱包中需要有足够FAB作为燃料，依此类推 ...
需要特别说明的是，亿币交易所是运行在一个特殊的区块链Kanban上的，Kanban以FAB作为燃料，因此，在交易所中交易任何币中，均仅消耗FAB作为燃料，如果交易所中没有足够的FAB燃料，无法执行任何操作。

2. 为什么在向交易所转币时，经常发生错误?

由于交易所需要确定转账与钱包的对应关系，向交易所转币只能一笔一笔的进行，即当一笔交易完成后，才能再转另一笔，比如您准备向交易所分别存BTC和ETH，你首先提交了一笔BTC存币, 需要等交易所收到这笔后再存另一笔ETH，否则会提示错误。通常完成一笔向交易所转币操作可能需要半个小时至一个小时。

3. 钱包里币已经扣掉了，为什么交易所没收到?

为了确保交易有效无误，交易链通常需要在原链确定了一定的块数后才认为交易有效，所以在存币后请耐心等待，通常需要半个小时至一个小时的时间，有时甚至两个小时，如果更长时间没收到，请检查存币按钮处是否出现了一个确认存币按钮，如果有，点击该按钮并输入钱包密码确认，交易所就立即收到存币了。

4. 为什么转USDT时，提示没有足够ETH?

亿币是去中心化钱包及交易所，一切操作都在链上进行，由于USDT是以太坊区块链上的代币，所以转发任何以太坊上的代币包括USDT都需向以太坊区块链支付燃料费，即使向交易所转币也一样；同理，转发EXG、DUSD等FAB区块链上的币，需要支付FAB燃料费，所以在转代币时，您的钱包里必须有对应的主链币。

5. 钱包里有足够的ETH，为什么向交易所转USDT没收到?

交易所的任何操作都需要在交易所预先充燃料(FAB)，不管向交易所充币还是在交易所执行买卖交易，交易所都必须有足够的燃料，需要特别强调的是，交易所内的一切交易都是以FAB作为燃料的，不管什么币交易。

6. 什么是加燃料，一般要加多少?

加燃料操作是向交易所加FAB，交易所的一切交易都是以FAB作为燃料的，在使用交易所之前，必须首先加燃料，否则，在交易所内什么都不能做，包括向交易所转币，具体需要加多少视您在交易所的交易频率，一般加几个FAB就可以用比较长一段时间。当然，如果您只用钱包，不用交易所，则不需加燃料。

7. 从交易所向钱包提币为什么没收到?

交易所区块链每半小时执行一次提币操作，而目的区块链有时可能比较繁忙，通常提币操作应在40分钟内完成，但因第三方链繁忙的原因，有时需要几个小时甚至更长时间。

8. 我想加燃料，从哪里获得FAB?
